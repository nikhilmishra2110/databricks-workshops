# Rubric: Workshop Readiness

| Area | Ready | Needs Work |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Outcomes | Outcomes are observable and audience-specific | Outcomes are vague or too broad |
| Timing | Agenda includes realistic hands-on time | Agenda is lecture-heavy or overpacked |
| Setup | Setup is tested from scratch | Setup assumes local knowledge |
| Data | Data is synthetic, small, and concept-aligned | Data is too large, sensitive, or distracting |
| Exercises | Exercises progress in difficulty | Exercises combine too many new concepts |
| Solutions | Solutions are tested and explained | Solutions are missing or untested |
| Facilitation | Common issues and hints are documented | Facilitator must improvise all support |
| Prompts | Prompts include context, inputs, and review criteria | Prompts are generic or hard to evaluate |

## Readiness Decision

- Ready: all areas are ready or have minor fixes
- Needs review: one or two important areas need work
- Not ready: setup, data, or solutions are untested
