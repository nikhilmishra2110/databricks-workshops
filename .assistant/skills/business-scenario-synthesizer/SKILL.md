# Business Scenario Synthesizer

Use this skill to turn a technical Databricks asset into a realistic business conversation.

## Steps

1. Identify the business persona.
2. Identify the decision they need to make.
3. Write natural-language questions they would ask.
4. Tie each question to a trusted table, metric view, or business term.
5. Mark expected answers as trusted, weak, or likely to fail.
6. Suggest metadata or modeling improvements for weak answers.

## Standards

- Keep questions realistic and business-friendly.
- Avoid technical table names in user-facing questions.
- Include questions that expose missing metadata or ambiguous definitions.
