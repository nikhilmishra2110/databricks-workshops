# Exercise 1: Use Case Framing

## Goal

Choose a workshop use case that is valuable, scoped, and feasible.

## Task

Review `../use-cases.md` and select one anchor use case.

Recommended: Field Sales Account Intelligence Copilot.

## Questions

1. Who is the user?
2. What business decision do they make?
3. What data do they need?
4. What output should the GenAI workflow produce?
5. How will you know the output is good?

## Expected Output

A short use case statement:

```text
For sales leaders and field reps, build an account intelligence workflow that identifies reorder risk, explains the reason, recommends next actions, and captures rep feedback.
```
