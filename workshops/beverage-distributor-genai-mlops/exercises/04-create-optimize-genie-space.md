# Exercise 4: Create And Optimize Genie Space

## Goal

Create a Genie space that answers trusted business questions.

## Steps

1. Use curated tables only.
2. Use prompt 3 from `../prompts/prompt-pack.md`.
3. Add business-friendly table and column descriptions.
4. Add sample questions from `../assets/genie-space-context.yml`.
5. Test at least five questions.
6. Record questions that fail or produce weak answers.

## Done When

- The space focuses on curated account intelligence tables.
- Ambiguous business terms are defined.
- At least five sample questions are tested.
- Weak answers become metadata or modeling improvements.

## Example Questions

- Which accounts are at highest reorder risk?
- Which products are constrained by low available inventory?
- Which promotions are driving repeat orders?
- Why should a sales rep contact this account?
